Industrial Water Management System

The IoT gives increased access to control of smart devices. Sensors on these smart devices measure certain types of data. Design an IoT-enabled system that will manage digitilaise the water usage. Use low-cost hardware to interface with sensors, collect the data on the cloud and determine when and how the industriers conusmed watered.

Factory-Verified Vehicle Access Control System

Factories send their goods vehicles out for transporting which gets checked at the check post of the economic zone to ensure verified vehicle access. This will be implemented by digitalising the verification and establishing a portal where factories can enter their vehicles and metadata which will then be automatically identified at the check post.
